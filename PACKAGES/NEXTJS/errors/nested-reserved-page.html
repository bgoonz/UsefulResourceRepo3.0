Nested Reserved Page

Why This Error Occurred

In your pages folder you nested a reserved page e.g.Â _app, _error, or _document which causes the page to not be used since they must be located directly under the pages folder.

Possible Ways to Fix It

Move the reserved pages directly under your pages folder so that they are picked up and used correctly.
