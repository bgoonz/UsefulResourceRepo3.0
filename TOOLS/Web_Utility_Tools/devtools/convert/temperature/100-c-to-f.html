100 degrees Celsius to Fahrenheit

100 degrees Celsius are equal to 212 degrees Fahrenheit:

100ºC = 212ºF

Calculation

The temperature T in degrees Fahrenheit (ºF) is equal to 100 degrees Celsius (ºC) times 9/5 plus 32:

T(ºF) = 100ºC × 9/5 + 32 = 212ºF
