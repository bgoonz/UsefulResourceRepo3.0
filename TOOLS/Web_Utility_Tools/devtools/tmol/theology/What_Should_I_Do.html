What should I do?

During the past history, there were many doctrines how a person should behave. Many were more reasonable and many were less reasonable. Here is another version:

1. I should take care of myself by maintaining all proper body functionalities (homeostasis).

2. I should take care of other people, animals and nature. This rule will also serve me.

In order to maintain proper body functions (homeostasis), do the following:

  • Breath air - always.
  • When you feel thirsty - drink water.
  • When you feel hungry - eat food.
  • When you feel tired - sleep.
  • When you feel hot - get cool.
  • When you feel cold - get warm.
  • When you feel pain - find the cause of the pain and take action to remove it.
