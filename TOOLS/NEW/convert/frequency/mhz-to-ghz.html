How to convert megahertz to gigahertz

1GHz = 1000MHz

or

1MHz = 0.001GHz

Megahertz to gigahertz formula

The frequency f in gigahertz (GHz) is equal to the frequency f in megahertz (MHz) divided by 1000:

f(GHz) = f(MHz) / 1000

Example

Convert 3 megahertz to gigahertz:

f(GHz) = 3MHz / 1000 = 0.003GHz
