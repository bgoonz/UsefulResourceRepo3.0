Event Details & Agenda

Our 'Kickstart Your Career in Development' series is focused on exploring what it takes to start your career as a front-end developer, back-end developer or full stack developer. Each hour-long event is led by a industry leader in development, who will guide you through coding fundamentals, industry trends and case studies on how some of the world's most renowned applications are made.

  1. Industry and Trends10 mins
  2. Case Studies in Development10 mins
  3. Career Possibilities & Development Roles20 mins
  4. Mapping Out a Career Transition15 mins
  5. Next Steps15 mins

We'll outline the importance of development in today's economy and trace the industry's history. We'll also clarify common misconceptions about development and identify emerging trends in the industry.
