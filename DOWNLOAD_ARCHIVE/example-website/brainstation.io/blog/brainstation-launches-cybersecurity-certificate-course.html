BrainStation Launches Cybersecurity Certificate Course

By Emily Peck December 8, 2020
Share

We’re excited to announce the launch of BrainStation’s Cybersecurity Certificate Course! 

The Cybersecurity Certificate Course is designed to provide individuals and organizations with a detailed understanding of how to protect mission-critical data and secure day-to-day operations. Taught by industry leaders, the course uses an applied, project-based learning model that ensures professionals gain hands-on experience implementing new security skills and knowledge.

“As businesses expand their online presence and increase their digital footprint, we’re proud to empower learners on how to protect their businesses’ critical information with this in-depth professional development course,” said Jason Field, BrainStation Founder and CEO.

Secure Your Assets and Data

In the era of big data, cybersecurity is increasingly important in protecting digital data from damage, theft, or attack. Digital data can include industry information, intellectual property, identifiable information, personal and protected health information, and more.

Neglecting to implement an effective cybersecurity strategy can leave businesses and organizations vulnerable to severe cybercrimes, including the loss of sensitive corporate and customer information and a disruption of service and internal systems. To understand the scope of this problem, Cybersecurity Ventures predicts cybercrime will cost the world more than $6 trillion annually by 2021, up from $3 trillion in 2015.

Digital leaders today must understand how security threats exploit our most commonly-used technologies. The Cybersecurity Certificate Course is for those who want a practical, leading-edge understanding of the threat landscape, security best practices, and industry techniques,” said Field.

What The Course Covers 

BrainStation’s Cybersecurity Certificate Course will give you an extensive understanding of cybersecurity strategy, concepts and techniques, including:

Cybersecurity Fundamentals
Gain in-depth knowledge of cybersecurity and the technology behind it. Understand industry security techniques and securing information through encryption, authentication, and authorization.

Threat Landscape
Develop a detailed understanding of threats including networking, social engineering, malware, and more. Learn how tools and industry best practices can be applied to defend against and to mitigate the impact of cyber threats.

Security Strategy & Risk Management
Apply your new skills to solve real business problems, protect your data, and improve your operating practices. Learn how to perform basic security audits, develop adaptive strategies and respond to threats. 

Being proactive about technology policies instills confidence and trust in your systems and allows you to focus on your organization’s purpose,” said Field.

Equip your business to innovate securely without slowing down. Enroll today to tap into a global network of instructors and students at some of the most innovative organizations in the world.
