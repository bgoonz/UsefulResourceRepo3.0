    UNIT 1

    Marketing Strategy Foundations

    You'll launch into the Digital Marketing course by learning foundational digital marketing concepts, including digital strategy, brand positioning, mobile marketing, web analytics, and understanding your audience. You'll learn how to use key SEO tools, complete effective keyword research, and define customer personas and journeys, enabling you to craft content and write copy that persuasively connects with your audience and improves your digital marketing efforts.

    • Google Chrome
    • Google Search
    KEY SKILLS:
    Digital Strategy
    Customer Journey
    Content & Copywriting
    Personas

    Understand and Define Your Customers

    Define customer personas and their customer journey as the foundation of your holistic online digital marketing strategy.

    Learn the Essentials of Copywriting

    Discover the essential inputs to all great copywriting and get hands-on practice writing truly persuasive copy.

    Complete Effective Research

    Learn how to apply qualitative and quantitative research to inform your digital marketing strategy.

    Apply Effective Content Marketing Strategies

    Learn how to combine evergreen and seasonal content to support your marketing objectives.

    UNIT 2

    Attracting & Engaging Audiences

    Search engines have emerged as one of the most powerful digital channels available to modern digital marketers by engaging targeted audiences with high purchase intent and driving them directly to your online business. In this Digital Marketing course, you will understand the differences between Search Engine Marketing (SEM) and Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and how to use both organic and paid traffic as part of your digital marketing strategy. With Google Ads, you'll get hands-on experience completing keyword research and designing effective ads that align with your strategy for this digital world.

    • Google Ads
    • Google Chrome
    • Google Search
    • HTML
    KEY SKILLS:
    SEO
    SEM
    Websites
    Structured Data

    Understand and Apply Effective SEO Strategies

    Learn how search engines work and get hands-on practice using Google's Keyword Planner to complete research and prioritize top keywords that drive your Search Engine Optimization (SEO).

    Drive Targeted Traffic With Paid Search

    Create a Google Ads strategy, including Pay-Per-Click, display, and retargeting ads, to meet your unique campaign goals. Get hands-on practice using Google Ads.

    Harness Websites & Landing Pages to Increase Conversions

    Learn how websites and landing pages fit into your conversion funnel and how to optimize them for your marketing strategy incorporating both Search Engine Optimization and Google Ads.

    UNIT 3

    Digital Marketing Analytics

    All online digital marketing campaigns generate detailed data. In this Digital Marketing course, you will understand how to harness and analyze your marketing data and digital analytics, and will unlock the ability to leverage data-driven insights to iterate and optimize your marketing strategy to maximize long-term results.

    • Google Analytics
    • Google Chrome
    KEY SKILLS:
    Optimization
    Reporting

    Discover the Essential Role Analytics Plays in Your Digital Marketing Strategy

    Understand how analytics, from tools like Google Analytics, informs marketing strategies, and why digital analytics has become an essential tool for Digital Marketers.

    Analyze and Leverage Critical Marketing Reports

    Learn how to access and use Google Analytics, including the key Audience, Acquisition, and Behavior reports used by marketers. Get hands-on practice navigating and using Google Analytics for a real website.

    UNIT 4

    Social Media Marketing

    Billions of consumers are on social media looking to be entertained and informed. Understand the unique qualities of the world's most popular social media platforms – including Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat, LinkedIn, Twitter, and TikTok – and learn to apply their unique capabilities to engage and grow your community with this powerful inbound marketing tool.

    • Facebook
    • Instagram
    • Snapchat
    • LinkedIn
    • Twitter
    • TikTok
    KEY SKILLS:
    Pixel Marketing
    Influencers

    Fundamental Social Media Marketing Strategy

    Learn the fundamentals of social media marketing, including how to apply online digital marketing strategy to the social media landscape. Understand the differences between organic and paid content and the different social media marketing strategies brands use to build engagement and awareness with social media in general.

    Social Media Platform Breakdown

    Go in-depth with the leading social media platforms by understanding the unique qualities of each platform and its users, why you should use each platform, the ad formats available, and how to create effective content within each platform's ecosystem. Leaving this Digital Marketing course, you'll have learned about the best digital marketing strategies for Facebook marketing (including Facebook ads), Instagram, Snapchat, LinkedIn, Twitter, and TikTok.

    Influencers & Social Media Planning

    Learn the nuances of influencer marketing, including what they are, how to categorize them, and how to engage them to market your product or service. You'll learn the step-by-step process for engaging social media influencers, including how to choose a platform, find an influencer, develop a contract, and more. You'll also learn how to budget and plan for your social media marketing efforts and track analytics to evaluate the outcomes.

    UNIT 5

    Email Marketing

    Despite its widespread ubiquity, email marketing is one of the few digital marketing channels brands can truly own, providing access to their audience on their own terms. Learn strategies to build an email list and email marketing campaigns, with hands-on practice using MailChimp to design effective emails and drip campaigns to drive a wide variety of digital marketing goals.

    • Mailchimp
    • Gmail
    • Google Chrome
    • Canva
    KEY SKILLS:
    Email Strategy

    Develop an Optimized Email Marketing Strategy for Your Brand and Marketing Goals

    Understand how Email Marketing fits within the digital marketing funnel, along with different types of email marketing campaigns and their objectives.

    Explore How Drip Campaigns Help You Achieve Your Digital Marketing Objectives

    Through live, interactive learning, learn how to craft effective email drip campaigns for a variety of practical digital marketing initiatives and inbound marketing strategies.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • How advanced is the Digital Marketing Course?

      Our in-person or online Digital Marketing course will give you an in-depth overview of essential digital marketing skills to help you get started in email marketing, paid social, Google Ads, SEO, and more. While extensive, the Digital Marketing course can be considered beginner-friendly.

    • Will I earn a Digital Marketing Certificate from this course?

      Yes, when you complete the course, you will earn your Digital Marketing certificate. Getting a digital marketing certification can boost your LinkedIn profile and resume, helping you stand out in the job market and can help you land a job as a Digital Marketing Specialist or Digital Marketing Manager, among other roles in the digital marketing industry.

    • What payment options do you offer?

      BrainStation offers some of the most competitive payment options for digital marketing training, with a range of flexible plans and scholarship opportunities.

      These include:

      • Monthly payment installments

        Allowing you to split your tuition into smaller monthly payments.

      • Employer sponsorship

        Get your tuition reimbursed by your employer.

      • Scholarships

        We offer a range of scholarships to make learning digital marketing more accessible.

    • Are there any prerequisites for this digital marketing course?

      BrainStation's Digital Marketing course content was created for those who are new to digital marketing or have experience with one aspect of marketing and are looking to learn new channels and techniques. There are no prerequisites for this course as it's suitable for those looking to brush up on their digital marketing skills or professionals who want to dive into something brand new.

    • What kind of backgrounds do people have that take this course?

      By enrolling in the Digital Marketing course, you can expect to learn from Instructors experienced in the field, and to collaborate with like-minded, ambitious professionals keen to boost their digital marketing skills.

      From an educational point of view, 80 percent of learners at BrainStation have a post-secondary education, with 20 percent having earned a Master's degree or PhD.

      Professional backgrounds vary and may include those who already work in marketing looking to update their skills, entrepreneurs who want to learn how to market their brands, and sales professionals who want to better understand digital marketing to work cross-functionally with their team.

    • What digital marketing tools do we learn?

      Throughout the Digital Marketing course, you will learn to work with tools that help you become a more efficient and effective digital marketer.

      You'll get hands-on experience with Google Ads, Google Analytics, and more, to help you build a dynamic and well-rounded digital marketing strategy.
